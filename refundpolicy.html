Entry Fee & Ticket Guidelines

Automatic Refunds
If an organizer cancels a lobby, or if technical system anomalies prevent a custom match room from launching, your ticket fee is returned instantly.
Voluntary Exits
Tickets are generally non-refundable once matches are actively running. Voluntary exits, disconnections, or late-joins are not eligible for refunds.
Wallet Cash Balance
Your deposited security wallet balances are held safely. Refund requests for accidental double payments are resolved quickly upon verification.
Promotional Credits
Welcome bonuses, affiliate referral commissions, and promotional vouchers can be spent on entry tickets but cannot be directly withdrawn.

Wallet Withdrawal Process

ROCKY AXIS ESPORTS ensures fast, hassle-free withdrawals from your wallet balance. Our financial framework guarantees:

  • Swift Processing Windows: Once approved, withdrawals are fast-tracked and completed within **24-48 business hours**.
  • Transaction Transparency: Withdrawal status updates, along with any necessary verification steps, are sent directly to your feed.
  • No Hidden Costs: Rocky Axis only deducts actual transaction fees charged by payment gateways (eSewa, Khalti, or mobile transfers).

Filing a Refund Claim

If you experience transaction anomalies, you can easily open an audit case:

  1. Submit a fast-track dispute report inside your ended lobby dashboard.
  2. Our admin panel audits lobby logs and screenshot proofs to verify the claim.
  3. Verified transaction errors are settled directly back to your player wallet within 24 hours.
