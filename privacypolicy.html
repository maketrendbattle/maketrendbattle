Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

At ROCKY AXIS ESPORTS, protecting your privacy and securing your personal credentials are our highest operational priorities. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, process, and safeguard your account parameters when participating in custom rooms, matches, and ledger payouts.

Privacy Assurance: By using our platform services, you acknowledge and consent to the data safeguarding systems outlined in this policy. All values are held with absolute transparency.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver automated lobby settlements, the platform registers several primary types of information:

Personal Information

  • Required Authentication Details: Account email, usernames, encrypted secure passwords.
  • Required Gamer Profile Parameters: Full names, custom avatar configurations, in-game Free Fire nicknames, and game UIDs.
  • Optional Contact References: Phone numbers (used solely for verification handshakes).

Automatically Processed Data

  • Arena Diagnostics: Submitted match screenshot proofs, game turnout ratios, and ballot verifier consensus parameters.
  • Platform Analytics: Device type characteristics, hardware thread parameters (to filter fraud clustering), and focus metrics.

We process your data strictly to secure game lobbies and protect financial settlements:

3. Sharing & Disclosure

ROCKY AXIS ESPORTS does not sell, rent, or trade your personal credentials. Your information is shared exclusively under strict safeguards:

  • Public Leaderboards: Usernames, game performance statistics, and won prizes are public to foster healthy competitive camaraderie.
  • Service Providers: Transaction details are processed securely through established gateway protocols (e.g., eSewa, Khalti, or mobile transfers) to verify deposit settlements.
  • Legal Mandates: Handshaked only if required by regulatory authorities to fulfill local compliance directives.

4. Data Security & Storage

We enforce modern organizational and technical security measures to protect player data:

  • Ledger Encryptions: Sensitive financial logs and transaction history details are encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  • Authentication Guards: Strict Firestore security rules prevent players from viewing or altering other players' private transaction histories.
  • Roster Safety: Personal telephone numbers and emails are hidden and are never visible on public matches feed pages.

5. Data Retention Limits

We retain credentials only as long as necessary to maintain active player profiles or satisfy auditing compliance requirements:

  • Account Records: Maintained for the lifetime of your active profile.
  • Financial Ledger Logs: Retained for a strict 7-year compliance duration to preserve audit trails.
  • Dispute Evidence Screenshots: Purged automatically after 90 days following match completion.

Rocky Axis tournament lobbies are not structured for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly compile information from young children. If you believe your child has registered an account, contact our support line for instant, permanent purging.
