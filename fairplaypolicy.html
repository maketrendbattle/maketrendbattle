Tournament Standards

All players must submit authentic outcomes. Collaborative match-fixing or coordinating lobby placements results in automatic validation holds.
Respectful Environment
Hate speech, toxic behavior, or team-killing inside custom matches is prohibited. We foster competitive camaraderie across our gaming hub.
